# Datasets

[Back to index](../README.md)

| Paper | Summary |
|-------|---------|
| [Open X-Embodiment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.08864) | Aggregates 1M+ robot demonstrations from 22 embodiments across 21 institutions into one dataset. Training on this diverse data significantly improves generalization over single-robot datasets. |
| [D4RL: Datasets for Deep Data-Driven Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.07219) | Standardized benchmark suite for offline RL with datasets of varying quality (random, medium, expert) across locomotion, navigation, and manipulation tasks. The standard offline RL benchmark. |
